Desert Gear: As a southern Californian, I’m surrounded by numerous hiking opportunities. Whether that’s the Los Padres national Forest, Angeles National Forest, San Bernardino National Forest, or even some parts of Inyo National Forest, these environments require specific gear (things that allow for making extra room for extra water). Because you’re often not dealing with cold temperatures, there’s little need to bring the heavier warm items, as you wouldn’t want to bring more than you need to. So, this set up will include the lighter items of my available options.Click on the pictures for more details.
